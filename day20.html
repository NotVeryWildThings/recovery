Day 20

6/14/2018

20 days.

It sure feels longer than that, not that it's a bad thing. At this point of my life I have a greater appreciation for the moments ahead, both expected and, to a greater extent, unexpected. The notion of a future feels like hope. Another day, another chance to right the wrongs, what an incredible gift.

Talking about gifts you, my friends and family, are the most wonderful gifts of them all. When all is said and done, at the end of each adventure, the best of them are always the ones in which I bonded with someone or nurtured an existing one. These bonds are what make me feel like a rich man in this world providing me with acceptance, self-worth and the purest of joys. I have resolved myself to emphasize this in all aspects of my social life and for that I feel unabashedly enlightened :)

But truly this latest nugget of self-discovery feels like a major stepping stone in what I would define as my maturity. It is as if I developed a new brain muscle that pulls me back on the right path when my mind starts to wander off. It is reshaping my social landscape and fills it with exciting new opportunities.

So here's to health and your company, And one for the lasses, Let's drink and be merry, all out of our glasses, Let's drink and be merry, bad thoughts to refrain, For we may or may not ever all be here again.
